About

Even before we were married, we enjoyed exploring new places and recipes together and capturing our best discoveries in photos. Pretty soon, we had much more photography and stories to treasure than could fit in a few albums and wonderful friends spread across the globe with whom to share them. So we created Table M – a site dedicated to some of our favorite foods and memories.

Although we currently live in San Diego, CA, we take every opportunity to travel. Our foods and trips reflect Ingo’s German and American heritage as well as Jane’s Russian Jewish roots.

Find us elsewhere at Ingo Muschenetz (LinkedIn) and Jane Muschenetz (Palm Frond Zoo).
