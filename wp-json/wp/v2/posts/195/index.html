Pear Sorbet<\/h3>
  • Servings: <\/strong>4<\/li>
  • Print<\/a><\/li><\/ul>
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    A wonderful easy pear sorbet.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ingredients<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
    • 5 ripe pears, cored and coarsely chopped<\/li>
    • \u00bd cup to \u00be cup superfine sugar (depending on pears)<\/li>
    • 3 tablespoons pear brandy<\/li>
    • 3-5 cardamom pods, ground<\/li>
    • pinch salt<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/div><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Directions<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
      1. Blend all together until mixture is smooth. Pour into ice cream freezer and freeze until firm.<\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
