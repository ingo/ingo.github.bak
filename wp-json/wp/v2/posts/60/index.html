Baked Beans with Buttered Toast<\/h3>
  • Servings: <\/strong>1<\/li>
  • Print<\/a><\/li><\/ul>
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ingredients<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
    • 1 cup baked beans<\/li>
    • 2 slices white bread<\/li>
    • butter<\/li>
    • Poached Sunny Side Up Eggs<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/div><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Directions<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
      1. Heat the beans. Toast the bread and spread with butter. Place eggs on place, place beans on side, and add bread.<\/li>
      2. Dish is best eaten when eggs are broken, and eggs and beans are sopped up with bread.<\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
