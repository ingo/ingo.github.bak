White Sangria<\/h3>
  • Servings: <\/strong>4<\/li>
  • Print<\/a><\/li><\/ul>
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    A great recipe for white wine Sangria.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ingredients<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
    • 2 bottles light white wine, on the sweet side (Pinot Grigio and a Moscato are what we last used)<\/li>
    • 1\/2 pkg frozen mango chunks<\/li>
    • 2 peaches (sliced)<\/li>
    • 1 lime (very thinly sliced, with rind on)<\/li>
    • 2 navel oranges (very thinly sliced–with rind on)<\/li>
    • 1\/2 cup peach schnapps<\/li>
    • 1\/2 cup brandy<\/li>
    • 1\/4 cup sugar<\/li>
    • ginger ale<\/li>
    • ice<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/div><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Directions<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
      1. Mix the sugar, brandy, schnapps and fruit together and let sit for several hours in the refrigerator.<\/li>
      2. Add the wine and top with ginger ale. Add ice if necessary.<\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
