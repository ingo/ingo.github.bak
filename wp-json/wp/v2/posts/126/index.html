A favorite nut recipe of Ilona
\n<\/span>
\n<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 2 1\/4 lb. assorted unsalted nuts (the fancy raw nut mix from tj’s which worked well and just added more pecans)<\/li>\n
  • 2 tblsp. chopped fresh rosemary<\/li>\n
  • 1\/2 tsp. cayenne<\/li>\n
  • 2 tsp. dark brown sugar<\/li>\n
  • 2 tsp. sea salt<\/li>\n
  • 1 tblsp. unsalted butter<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Toast nuts until light golden brown (about 10 min). Melt butter. Combine in a bowl with other ingredients, add nuts, toss to coat, serve warm.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
