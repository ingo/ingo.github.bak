Ingo’s Dad’s German Potato Salad. This would often accompany Bratwurst.<\/span>
\n<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 4 lbs. small potatoes<\/li>\n
  • salt to taste<\/li>\n
  • 6 oz. bacon, chopped and fried<\/li>\n
  • 1\/2 c white wine vinegar<\/li>\n
  • 1\/4 c sugar<\/li>\n
  • 1\/2 c boiling water with 1 bouillon cube added<\/li>\n
  • 1\/2 tsp. black pepper<\/li>\n
  • 1 tsp. dried mustard or 1 tsp. good deli mustard<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Boil potatoes with 1 tsp. salt until easily pierced with a fork. Drain, peel, and cut into 1\/2 inch slices.<\/p>\n

    Combine vinegar, sugar, water, salt, black pepper and mustard and pour over the still warm potatoes. Add the chopped fried bacon and toss lightly.<\/p>\n

    This salad is good warm or cold.<\/p>\n

    Variation<\/h3>\n

    Add two hard boiled eggs and two dill pickles, coarsely chopped.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
