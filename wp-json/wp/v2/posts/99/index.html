\n[singlepic=4196,300,450]<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

4 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 2 lbs carrots<\/li>\n
  • 1 yellow onion<\/li>\n
  • 1 bay leaf<\/li>\n
  • 1 green bell pepper<\/li>\n
  • ketchup or tomato paste<\/li>\n
  • salt<\/li>\n
  • pepper<\/li>\n
  • a little water<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Peel carrots and shred in food processor. Dice yellow onion and fry in saut\u00e9 pan with vegetable and olive oil. Add shredded carrots, bay leaf and a little water, cover and cook on high until steaming.<\/p>\n

    Peel and seed green bell-pepper and cube in large cubes add to carrots. Stir and add salt, pepper to taste. Turn down heat, cover and cook for 20 minutes.<\/p>\n

    Add ketchup or tomato paste, stir and cover for 5 min, turn off heat and leave covered until ready to serve.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
