Yield<\/h3>\n

8 burgers (2 per person)<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 1 1\/2 lbs. ground lamb<\/li>\n
  • 8 small Hamburger Buns<\/li>\n
  • cheddar cheese<\/li>\n
  • minced shallots<\/li>\n
  • Peach Chutney<\/li>\n
  • mayonnaise<\/li>\n
  • salt<\/li>\n
  • pepper<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Mix the lamb with salt and pepper to taste and form into 8 small patties. Grill or broil until done on one side, then flip and cover with a slice of cheddar cheese.<\/p>\n

    While the burgers are cooking, cut the buns in half and toast, if desired. Spread the base with mayonnaise. Place the finished patty on top, cover with a large dollop of peach chutney and sprinkle with the mined shallots. Serve immediately.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
