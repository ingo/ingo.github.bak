A very simple, very German salad. Great with spicy foods.<\/span>
\n
\n[singlepic=4193,300,450]<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 2-3 cucumbers<\/li>\n
  • Kosher salt<\/li>\n
  • Sugar<\/li>\n
  • Distilled vinegar<\/li>\n
  • Pepper<\/li>\n
  • Caraway seeds (optional)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Peel the cucumbers. Slice wafer-thin using a mandoline. Place in a colander, spinkle with salt, mix, and leave to drain for at least an hour.<\/p>\n

    Rinse briefly, drain. Place in bowl, and add sugar and vinegar (start with 1 tblsp vinegar, 1 tsp sugar, then modify to taste. Add pepper and caraway seeds (Jane doesn’t like them, but Ingo finds them indespensible, and they are typically German).<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
