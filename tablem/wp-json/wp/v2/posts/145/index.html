Yield<\/h3>\n

4 servings<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 2-4 pitas, cut into wedges (approximately 10 wedges per pita)<\/li>\n
  • 1 tbsp olive oil<\/li>\n
  • Seasoning (Pepper Plant spice mix from San Luis Obispo is a favorite)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place cut pitas into a bowl. Drizzle with oil and salt. Toss to coat. Place on cookie sheet and place in oven for 10-15 minutes until golden (watch burning!). Remove from oven. Toss with favorite seasoning and serve.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
