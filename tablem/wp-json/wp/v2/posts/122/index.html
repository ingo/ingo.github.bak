An easy-to-make sauce for shrimp or crab claws. It’s also good prepared with fresh dill or thyme instead of tarragon.
\n<\/span>
\n<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 1 cup ketchup<\/li>\n
  • 3 tablespoons grated onion<\/li>\n
  • 3 tablespoons prepared horseradish<\/li>\n
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice<\/li>\n
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh tarragon<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Whisk all ingredients in medium bowl to blend. Cover and refrigerate sauce at least 1 hour or up to 2 days.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
