This red pepper paste has a nice texture and flavor from the addition of walnuts. Serve it with pita chips, crackers, or as a spread on sandwiches.<\/span>
\n
\n[singlepic=4379,300,450]<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 4 red bell peppers<\/li>\n
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts<\/li>\n
  • 2 cloves garlic<\/li>\n
  • salt<\/li>\n
  • Juice of 1\/2 to 1 lemon<\/li>\n
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Heat broiler and roast peppers until charred on all sides. Place in paper bag until cool, then peel, seed and de-vein. Using the steel blade, blend to a paste in food processor, and then mix in remaining ingredients. Pulse in processor briefly, then adjust seasoning to taste.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
