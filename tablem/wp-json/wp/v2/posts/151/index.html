The cranberry sauce of Ingo’s Dad.<\/span>
\n
\n[singlepic=4192,300,450]<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 1 navel orange<\/li>\n
  • 1 (12-oz) bag fresh cranberries<\/li>\n
  • 2\/3 cup sugar<\/li>\n
  • 2 tbsp. Jack Daniels<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Finely grate 2 teaspoons zest from orange. Cut away and discard peel and pith from orange, then cut sections free from membranes.<\/p>\n

    Pulse cranberries with zest, orange sections, and sugar in a food processor until finely chopped. Add whiskey. Chill, covered, at least 2 hours to allow flavors to develop.<\/p>\n
