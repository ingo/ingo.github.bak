Sangria<\/h3>
  • Servings: <\/strong>4<\/li>
  • Print<\/a><\/li><\/ul>
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    A great recipe for Sangria.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ingredients<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
    • 1 bottle red wine (Spanish Red. Nothing too dry)<\/li>
    • At least 1 1\/4 cups sugar syrup (1\/2 sugar, 1\/2 water)<\/li>
    • 2 cups apple juice (not cider)<\/li>
    • 1 1\/4 cups silver rum<\/li>
    • 1\/4 cup Cointreau or Triple Sec<\/li>
    • juice of two limes<\/li>
    • 2 cinnamon sticks<\/li>
    • 1 orange, chopped<\/li>
    • ice<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/div><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Directions<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
      • Mix the syrup, rum, Cointreau, lime juice, orange together.<\/li>
      • Cover and refrigerate.<\/li>
      • 2 hours before serving, add wine and cinnamon. Serve over ice. If storing left-overs, remove cinnamon sticks.<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n
