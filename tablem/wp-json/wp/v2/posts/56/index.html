A favorite lunch-time dish of Tante Traudl.<\/span>
\n<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

2 cups<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • Fleckerl Noodles<\/li>\n
  • Smoked Ham<\/li>\n
  • 2 eggs<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Cook the noodles in water until tender. Drain.<\/p>\n

    Cook the ham in 3-4 cups of water. Drain, reserving water. Cut the ham into medium-sized pieces, and then grind coarsely into a roasting pan. Cover with the drained noodles. Add the eggs to the reserved cooking water, mix and then pour over the ham\/noodle mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours until golden.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
