Cranberry Margarita<\/h3>
  • Servings: <\/strong>4<\/li>
  • Print<\/a><\/li><\/ul>
    <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Fantastic margarita recipe.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Ingredients<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
    • 1 1\/4 cup cranberry juice<\/li>
    • 1\/2 cup sugar (or more, depending on the sweetness of the juice)<\/li>
    • 1 1\/2 cup fresh\/frozen whole cranberries, rinsed<\/li>
    • 3\/4 cup fresh squeezed lime juice<\/li>
    • 3\/4 cup tequila<\/li>
    • 1\/2 cup Grand Marnier or Triple Sec<\/li>
    • Ice<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      <\/div><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      Directions<\/h4><\/p>\n\n\n\n
      1. Pour half of mixture in the blender and fill rest with ice. Blend. Repeat with second half of mixture. Makes minimum of two blender batches.<\/li><\/ol>\n\n\n\n
