A very simple recipe but tasty way to prepare green beans<\/span>
\n<\/p>\n

Yield<\/h3>\n

4 servings<\/p>\n

Ingredients<\/h3>\n
    \n
  • 1 lb green beans, ends removed<\/li>\n
  • 1 cup chicken stock<\/li>\n
  • pinch of salt<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Directions<\/h3>\n

    Place chicken stock in medium sized saucepan. Bring to simmer. When simmering, place green beans in pot and cover. Let cook 5 minutes, or until just tender. Remove and sprinkle with salt. Serve.<\/p>\n

    Alterate Version: Drop green beans into ice water. Drain. Saute a minced shallot in a little oil. Toss in green beans and saute until warmed through.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
